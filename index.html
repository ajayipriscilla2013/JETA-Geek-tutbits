Our Core Features

Features

Academic Status Assessment

From the moment you subscribe, we assess your academic status and history to understand your current standing. This allows us to create a tailored study plan specific to your strengths and areas for improvement.

Parent/Guardian Involvement

We believe in keeping your support system informed. Regular progress updates are shared with your parent or guardian, providing peace of mind as you work toward academic success.

Streamlined Academic Journey

With access to your course registration and school calendar, we manage and prioritize key academic milestones. This way, you can stay prepared and avoid last-minute stress before exams and tests.

University Portal Monitoring

By accessing your University portal (with your permission), we track key metrics such as your grades, test scores, and attendance. Our goal is to catch any red flags early and help you make timely improvements.

Course Compact Assistance

We use your course compacts to align our tutoring and mentoring efforts with your course objectives. By following the specific guidelines of your registered courses, we ensure all assistance we provide is relevant to your academic needs.

Dedicated Course Mentoring

We break down complex course material into manageable learning goals, helping you better understand course content, improve your grades, and stay ahead in your studies.
